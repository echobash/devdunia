ℹ HOW IT WORKS INFO
1

Requires Chrome on Android and a secure context (HTTPS or localhost). Desktop browsers do not support Web NFC.

2

Press Start Scan and bring an NFC tag close to the back of your device. Your browser will ask for permission.

3

Supported record types are auto-parsed: text, URL, JSON MIME, and raw byte payloads shown in hex.

4

All data is processed 100% locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
