Privacy Policy — Stripe Reports (by echobash)

Effective Date: November 2025

Stripe Reports (by echobash) is a Chrome Extension developed by Ali Anwar. It does not collect, store, or share any personal or financial data from users.

The extension stores your Stripe Secret Key securely in Chrome's local storage. This key is used only to make direct HTTPS requests to https://api.stripe.com to fetch your Stripe account data (charges, payouts, refunds, etc.). No data is transmitted to any external server other than Stripe’s official API.

The extension operates entirely client-side and does not use analytics, tracking, or remote code.

For questions or feedback, contact the developer at github.com/echobash.
